------------
Snow-War 2.1
------------

Snow-War is half-life modification where you use snowballs instead weapons.

Please read the swmanual.chm for more information.

http://snowwar.hlgaming.com/
------------------------------------------------
UNISTALLATION:

You can either use uninstall.exe located in snow\ directory, or simply delete the entire folder.

KNOWN BUGS:

-Snowballs don't always make sound when they hit something
-Watersplash effect doesn't always appear
-bots sometimes get stuck
-bots run round and round the flag in CTF
-HL Deathmach models are seen in player options menu
