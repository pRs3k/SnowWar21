This map contains 13 maps for Half-Life Snow-War 2.0 mod.
Most of these are from older releases, except sw_industrial3 and sw_snowvalley.


Maps included:
ctf_riverside
kotm_fort
sw_backyard
sw_badtaste1_snowfall
sw_cool
sw_cream
sw_creambase
sw_ferret
sw_frostbite
sw_industrial3
sw_lake
sw_playgroundII
sw_snowvalley


Unzip to your snow\ directory.

for example, to: C:\Steam\SteamApps\player\half-life\snow
