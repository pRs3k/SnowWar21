Name:
	kotm_fort

Abstract:
	A small, 4-6 player fortress map. The top of the keep is a vital
	point in the map, and it will be the "king of the mountain" 			capture spot. This map is kept small in order to minimize 			r_speeds (which are only borderline at 2 locations), and to 		encourage conflict.
